Frequently Asked Questions

Our center specializes in comprehensive ophthalmology services, including eye exams, cataract surgery, glaucoma treatment, and more. We also provide advanced vascular access care, such as dialysis access management and vascular interventions.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office directly or using the appointment booking feature on our website. Walk-ins are welcome for urgent care needs.

Yes, we are equipped to handle eye and vascular emergencies. Our team is available 24/7 to provide immediate care for urgent situations.

We accept most major insurance plans. For specific inquiries, please contact our office, and our staff will assist you with your insurance coverage and billing questions.

For your first visit, please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or referral letters.
